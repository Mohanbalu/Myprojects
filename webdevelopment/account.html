An account refers to a user's personal or organizational profile on a specific platform. These accounts allow users to create, share, and engage with content within the social network. Different social media platforms may have varying features and terms for accounts, but here are some general points regarding types of social media accounts:


Types of Accounts:

  1. Private Account
  2. Business or Brand Account
  3. Verified Account
  4. Community or Group Account
  5. Professional Account
  6. Joint or Shared Account
  7. Anonymous or Pseudonymous Account:
  8. Fan or Fanpage Account
